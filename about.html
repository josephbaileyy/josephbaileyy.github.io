Research

AM CVn binaries

Helium-rich ultracompact binaries in which a white dwarf accretes from a degenerate donor, with orbital periods between roughly 5 and 65 minutes. They are among the strongest known sources of millihertz gravitational waves and key verification targets for the LISA mission.
